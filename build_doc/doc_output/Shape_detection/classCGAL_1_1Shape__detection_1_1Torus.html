template<class Traits>
class CGAL::Shape_detection::Torus< Traits >

Torus implements Shape_base.

The torus is represented by the symmetry axis, its center on the axis, and the major and minor radii.

Template Parameters
Traitsmust be a model of EfficientRANSACTraits with the additional requirement for tori (see EfficientRANSACTraits documentation).
